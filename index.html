About Us

UNESCO Arts & Sports Club is located in Pilakkottupadam Ward of Karulai Panchayat, Nilambur, Malappuram district. Established in 2013 with the aim of encouraging and nurturing the artistic and sporting talents of the youth of Pilakkottupadam, the club is a collective driven by social commitment. Our objective is to work together for the welfare and betterment of the community. Over the years, UNESCO Club has successfully carried out numerous activities for the benefit of the locality, including various charitable initiatives, free medical camps, football tournaments, and blood donation camps
