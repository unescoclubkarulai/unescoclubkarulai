About Us

UNESCO Arts & Sports Club is a premier organization located in Pilakkottupadam Ward of Karulai Panchayat, near Nilambur in Malappuram district. Established in 2013, we are widely recognized as the best club in Karulai, dedicated to encouraging and nurturing the artistic and sporting talents of our youth.

More than just a sports team, we are a collective driven by social commitment. Our objective is to work together for the welfare and betterment of the community in Pilakkottupadam and Karulai. Over the years, UNESCO Club has successfully carried out numerous charitable initiatives, free medical camps, and football tournaments, cementing our reputation as the best club in Malappuram for social service and sports excellence.
